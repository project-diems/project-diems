Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies

Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies, Aurangabad a private institute accredited by AICTE the college. The college provides 24 UG and PG courses in the disciplines of Engineering, Management. The flagship courses of the institute are the B.Tech program across 5 specializations and an MBA program. M.Tech course is also available. The total course fee for MBA at DIEMS is about INR 1,62,000 whereas, for the B.Tech program, the course fee is about INR 4,00,000. The college accepts JEE-Mains and MHT-CET for admission to the B.Tech program and CAT / MAT / CMAT scores are accepted for MBA program. However, candidates need to qualify in GATE to get enrollment in the M.Tech degree course.
